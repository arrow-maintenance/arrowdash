* **Apache Arrow 10th anniversary:** The project celebrated 10 years since its first commit in February 2016, reflecting on its growth as a universal standard for columnar data. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:ANNOUNCE%20Apache%20Arrow%20is%2010%20years%20old)

* **Release candidate provenance and security:** Discussion on tightening release procedures, including mandatory SHA hashes in vote emails and ensuring all artifacts are hosted on ASF-controlled infrastructure to prevent artifact substitution. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:Release%20candidate%20provenance%20question)

* **vgi-rpc — typed Arrow RPC for Python:** Introduction of a new RPC framework built directly on Arrow IPC that offers typed proxies and shared-memory transport without gRPC or Protobuf dependencies. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:DISCUSS%20Introducing%20vgi-rpc%20%E2%80%94%20typed%20Arrow%20RPC%20for%20Python%20(and%20soon%20other%20languages))

* **IPC format extensions:** Proposal to extend the IPC format with `ApplicationData` on messages and a new `Empty` message type to support custom metadata and improved stream signaling. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:IPC%20ApplicationData%20and%20IPC%20Empty%20Message%20Type%20Discussion%20-%20Document)

* **TIME support for 24:00:00:** Debate on whether Arrow's Time types should support "24:00:00" to ensure full data fidelity when round-tripping data from databases like PostgreSQL and DuckDB. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:TIME%20support%20for%20240000)

* **Equivalence of IPC file and stream formats:** Technical discussion on whether IPC files should be strictly readable as streams by skipping the header and how to handle potential metadata discrepancies in the footer. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:Discuss%20Equivalence%20of%20IPC%20file%20and%20stream%20formats)

* **Deprecation of legacy components:** Proposals to deprecate the Feather V1 reader/writer and the `pyarrow.gandiva` module due to low usage and high maintenance burdens. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:Discuss%20Deprecate%20pyarrow.gandiva)

* **AI-generated contribution policy:** A new proposal suggests that contributors must disclose if mailing list posts or questions are generated using AI to maintain transparency within the community. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:DISCUSS%20AI-generated%20contributions)

* **Arrow 23.0.1 maintenance release:** The community approved and released version 23.0.1 to address 28 resolved issues and improve release verification logging. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:VOTE%20Release%20Apache%20Arrow%2023.0.1%20-%20RC0)
