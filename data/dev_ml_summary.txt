* **C Data Interface nul bytes:** Technical discussion on how to handle field names containing nul bytes in the C FFI, highlighting discrepancies between Flatbuffers and C-style strings. [Thread](https://lists.apache.org/list?dev@arrow.apache.org:lte=2M:Spec%20clarification%20on%20field%20names)
